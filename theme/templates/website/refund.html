TopGrade Learn Today, Lead Tomorrow

We strive to deliver the highest quality learning experience through our programs, workshops, and internships. Before enrolling, please read our refund terms carefully, as they define our official policy.

What We Do

Top Grade Innovation is a leading ed-tech platform dedicated to empowering students and professionals with in-demand skills for career success. We bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world industry requirements through hands-on training, live interactive sessions, and placement-driven programs.

Our personalized learning experiences help learners gain practical exposure and build confidence in fields like technology, management, design, and healthcare, preparing them to excel in today's competitive job market.

Workshop

All payments made toward workshops are non-refundable and non-transferable. Once enrolled, no refunds or credits will be issued for cancellations, absences, or non-participation.

Courses and Internship Programs

All Payments made for online courses, training programs, or internships are non-refundable once completed. Learners should thoroughly review course information, schedules, and prerequisites before confirming their enrollment.

If you are unable to continue due to valid reasons such as health or scheduling conflicts, you may transfer your enrollment to a future batch upon payment of a nominal administrative fee. All transfer requests are subject to management approval and program availability.
