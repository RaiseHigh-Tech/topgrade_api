PRIVACY POLICY

TopGrade Learn Today, Lead Tomorrow

At Top Grade Innovation, accessible via https://www.topgradeinnovation.com, protecting the privacy of our learners and visitors is one of our top priorities. This Privacy Policy explains the types of information we collect, how we record and manage that information, and the ways in which we use it to provide and improve our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide during registration, program enrollment, or communication with our support team. This may include your name, email address, phone number, educational background, and payment details.

Additionally, when you access our website or app, we may collect technical data such as your IP address, device information, browser type, and cookies to improve performance and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used primarily to deliver and manage your courses, process payments, provide technical support, and communicate updates or offers related to our programs.

We may also use your data for analytics, quality assurance, and compliance with legal or regulatory obligations. Top Grade Innovation will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Sharing of Information

We may share limited information with trusted service providers, including payment gateways, hosting partners, or placement partners, who assist in operating our Platform or providing course-related services.

All such third parties are contractually obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of your data in accordance with applicable laws.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze platform performance.

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not function properly as a result.

5. Data Security

We implement strong administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

These measures include encrypted communication, secure data storage, and restricted employee access.

While we strive to maintain the highest standards of security, we cannot guarantee absolute protection against all threats or breaches.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by applicable law.

Once the retention period expires, your data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, withdraw consent, or object to certain processing activities.

Requests may be sent to support@topgradeinnovations.com, and we will act upon them in accordance with the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Platform is intended for users aged eighteen (18) years or older.

If we become aware that information from minors has been inadvertently collected, we will promptly remove such data from our systems.

9. Policy Updates

Top Grade Innovation may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws.

The revised version will be posted on our website with an updated effective date.

Continued use of our Platform after any modification constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
